Gladys Mae West

Gladys Mae West was born in October 27, 1930 in USA. She has a huge contributions to mathematical modeling of the shape of the Earth.

And her work on the development of satellite geodesy models, which were later incorporated into the Global Positioning System (GPS).

West was inducted into the United States Air Force Hall of Fame in 2018. West was awarded the Webby Lifetime Achievement Award for the development of satellite geodesy models.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopperwas born December 9, 1906 USA, New York. Died January 1, 1992 (aged 85)

She was a Computer programmer, She invented the first computer compiler, a program that translates written instructions into codes that computers read directly.

She joined the U.S. Navy during World second world War. After the war she continued to work in computing with her team, which led her to co-develop the COBOL, one of the earliest standardized computer languages learn more
