Charles Babbage

Charles Babbage was born 26 December 1791 London, England died 18 October 1871(aged 80)

A mathematician, Philosopher. known as "father of the computer", he was inventor and mechanical engineer,

He originated the concept of a digital programmable computer. He is credited with inventing the first mechanical computer, the Difference Engine, that eventually led to more complex electronic designs, though all the essential ideas of modern computers are to be found in his Analytical Engine, programmed using a principle openly borrowed from the Jacquard loom. learn more
