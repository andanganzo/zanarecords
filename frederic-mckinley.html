Frederick McKinley Jones

Frederick McKinley Jones was born on May 17, 1893, Cincinnati , Ohio died on February 21, 1961 (aged 67).

His carreer was Mechanical engineer He invented the first successful system for mobile refrigeration.

His invention eliminated the far less effective use of ice and salt to preserve foods for transport, greatly extending the distance over which food could be successfully delivered. learn more
