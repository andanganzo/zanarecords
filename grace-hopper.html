Grace Hopper

Grace Hopperwas born December 9, 1906 USA, New York. Died January 1, 1992 (aged 85)

She was a Computer programmer, She invented the first computer compiler, a program that translates written instructions into codes that computers read directly.

She joined the U.S. Navy during World second world War. After the war she continued to work in computing with her team, which led her to co-develop the COBOL, one of the earliest standardized computer languages learn more
